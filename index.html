About Me

Hey, I’m Conner — an aviation photographer and enthusiast based in South Africa.

I created Conner Aviation to share my passion for aircraft, flight, and everything in between. From photographing planes on approach to explaining complex aviation concepts, I want this site to be a hub for aviation lovers and curious minds alike.

Whether you're just starting out or a fellow avgeek, I hope you find this site inspiring and informative. I am the proud owner of the AveNav brand with the name AveNav meaning Ave-Aviation and Nav-Navigation. I aim to explore the world through flight and meet people with the same passion.
